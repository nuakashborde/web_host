Electricity for Everyone

Technology to empower rural electrification in Cameroon and South Sudan

1. Background

600 million people in Sub-Saharan Africa have no access to electricity.

Most of these people live in rural areas, >10km from the national grid.In many cases, building a minigrid is faster and cheaper than waiting for the national grid to reach these areas.

The more minigrids that local power producers can deploy, the more people can get electricity.

But minigrid developers are facing a challenge: Poor Metering Infrastructure
