Resolution was to write something everyday and to get something coded every day. Put it on the website as well.

Regardless, I'm going to try to make some resolutions for my upcoming year.

Years/weeks/months are all arbitrary, and starting somewhere is ultimately the most important part. Maybe mentally it's easier to get rid of the "I should have started earlier" worries by just using the new year to clean the slate.
